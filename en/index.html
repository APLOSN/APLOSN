APLOSN

APLOSN is the Association for the Promotion of Free, Open Source, and Digital Sovereignty in France

Who we are ?

We are an association committed to a free, ethical, and sovereign digital world. Our mission: to promote the use, development, and dissemination of free software and open-source solutions.

In response to the growing challenges of digital sovereignty, we raise awareness among the public, businesses, educational institutions, and local authorities about alternatives that respect digital freedoms. We defend users' rights and support initiatives aimed at strengthening technological independence in France.

Our actions focus on several key areas:

  • Raising awareness and educating about the importance of free software, cybersecurity, and open source;
  • Organizing events: conferences, workshops, training sessions, and thematic meetups;
  • Providing guidance and training for a digital world that is ethical, sustainable, and responsible.

We have also formed an active and structured Capture The Flag (CTF) team to:

  • Participate in national and international competitions;
  • Share technical knowledge in a spirit of mutual aid and self-improvement;
  • Highlight emerging talent within a legal and free-software-friendly framework.

Together, we strive for a freer, fairer, and more user-respecting digital future.
